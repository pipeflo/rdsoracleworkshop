Manual Backups

In Amazon RDS, Manual backups are called Snapshots, you can create one following the next steps:

  1. Find the RDS Oracle created instance under the Databases list and click on the DB Instance name.

  2. On the Instance details page, click the Actions button at the top right corner and then click Take snapshot.

  3. On the “Take DB Snapshot” page, enter a Snapshot name and then click Take Snapshot.

  4. After clicking Take snapshot you are redirected to the Snapshots page, you can also find this page by clicking Snapshots at the RDS Console menu on the left.

    Now please wait a few minutes for the Snapshot to be Available, you can confirm this by looking at the Status value.

Congratulations!! You have created your firts manual backup (Snapshot), in the following section you will restore a new instance from this Snapshot.
